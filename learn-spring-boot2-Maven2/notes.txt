• Types of logs 
	○ Trace 
		§ Prints everything
	○ Debug
		§ Prints a lot more information
	○ Info
		§ Prints everything a trace level
	○ Warning 
		§ Same as error with more info
	○ Error 
		§ Print only errors and exceptions
	○ Off
		§ Turn off all logging
